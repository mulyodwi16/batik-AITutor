# Sekar Jagad Motif

## Origin

The Sekar Jagad motif represents a broader vision of batik traditions that extend beyond a single village or region, celebrating the diversity of Indonesian batik heritage.

## Visual Characteristics

The Sekar Jagad motif features intricate floral and geometric patterns that create a rich, densely decorated surface. The design incorporates multiple layered elements that work together to create visual complexity and harmony.

## Meaning and Philosophy

Sekar Jagad embodies the concept of beauty and diversity that captivates and enchants all those who behold it. It celebrates the remarkable variety and richness found in nature and in human creative expression.

## Cultural Context

The name "Sekar Jagad" is derived from a combination of two languages:
- **KAART** (Dutch) = map
- **JAGAD** (Javanese) = world

This motif symbolizes the remarkable diversity and richness of batik traditions found throughout Indonesia and across the entire world. It represents the concept of unity in diversity, celebrating the varied cultural heritage and different batik expressions that exist globally while remaining connected through shared artistic traditions.

The motif exemplifies how batik serves as a universal language connecting different regions, cultures, and artistic perspectives within the Indonesian archipelago and beyond.
